Warm-You-Up Hot Chocolate

This delicious, not-too-sweet hot chocolate is richly flavored and delicately seasoned with spices.

Ingredients:

  1. 1/4 cup baking cocoa
  2. 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  3. 1 cup boiling water
  4. 2 teaspoons Gustus Vitae spicy chocolate cinnamon cane sugar
  5. Dash ground cloves or nutmeg
  6. 3 cups milk
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Whipped cream
  9. Whole cinnamon sticks

Directions:

  1. In a small saucepan, mix cocoa and sugar; stir in water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cook 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  2. Add spicy chocolate cinnamon sugar and cloves; stir in milk. Simmer 5 minutes (do not boil). Whisk in vanilla. Pour hot chocolate into mugs; top with whipped cream. Use cinnamon sticks for stirrers.

Nutrition Facts:

1 cup: 159 calories, 6g fat (3g saturated fat), 18mg cholesterol,
81mg sodium, 20g carbohydrate (16g sugars, 2g fiber), 7g protein.

Total Time:

Prep/Total Time: 10 min.

Makes: 4 servings
